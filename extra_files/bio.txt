Aleah is a writer hailing from Allentown, Pennsylvania. She has won numerous awards for both her fiction and non-fiction pieces. Aleah's stories have been published in <i> Hobart, The Doctor T.J., Eckleburg Review, Gone Lawn, Gigantic, and South Loop Review </i>. After spending a year researching the arts of Bonesetting on a Fullbright in Mongolia. Aleah is now getting her MFA in creative writing from the University of Alabama.
